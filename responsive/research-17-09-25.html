Personal Notes

  • Bootstrap: Quick setup, beginner-friendly, components easy to use.
  • TailwindCSS: Flexible, but learning curve is steeper due to utility classes.
  • Jekyll: Ideal for blogs; requires Ruby installation.
  • Hugo: Fast, minimal configuration, standalone binary.
  • GSAP: Advanced animations, professional quality, slightly steep learning curve.
  • Anime.js: Lightweight animations, simple implementation for small projects.

Critical Reflection

For CSS frameworks, I prefer Bootstrap for quick prototyping, but TailwindCSS is great for highly customized designs. For animations, GSAP offers professional control, while Anime.js is suitable for small projects. Regarding static generators, Hugo is my choice for large sites due to speed, and Jekyll for simple blogs. The combination of these tools balances speed, flexibility, and minimal dependencies.
